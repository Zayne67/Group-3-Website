Thunder Road Pizza


By: Jules Andrei Morada

Thunder Road Pizza & Grill is a full-service, family-friendly authentic Italian restaurant with American traditional steak twist located in the most touristic area of Umm Al Quwain. The restaurant celebrates Italy and its culinary diversity, combining the most authentic flavours along with traditional American steaks to give the foodies a truly unique range in the menu. The restaurant satisfies guests craving for great-tasting Italian pastas, pizzas, healthy salads, soups as well as juicy steaks and grills. Thunder Road Pizza & Grill has brought a fresh take to Italian casual cuisine and continues to honor its commitment to saying "no" to processed ingredients and saying "yes" to innovation and novelty, which is the secret to its success.
