By: Christian Jericson Vestil

Simplicity is central to Italian and Tuscan cuisine. At Villa Toscana, the food bridges tradition and elegance, serving hand-crafted, regional specialties from Tuscany, Umbria, and Emilia-Romagna. The restaurant's design, inspired by a Tuscan villa, creates the feel of visiting the home of an Italian noble at his summer residence where family and friends meet, welcomed by the chef-patron.
